Intro:

As the name suggests a push day involes pushing movements either above you, from you, or below you, the primary muscle groups involved in a push day include the chest, shoulders and also the triceps. In this page you will find 4 simple but effective exercises that will help you get started for an efficient but intense workout!

1. Bench Press

The bench press is one of the most iconic movements of bodybuilding, and its very simple to do. Simply lay on your back and bring the bar down to your chest and then push straight up and lock out your elbows.
